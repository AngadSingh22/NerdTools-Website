Guest Login


Human brains are wired in unique ways because of which there are differences in how cognitive skills develop. The CLASS (Cognitive Learning Ability Screening Score) is a digital, interactive and fun tool that provides a comprehensive profile of a child's strengths and difficulties in numerous domains required for reading and numeracy. CLASS is tailored for ages 5-18 (grades 1-12) and is available in English and Hindi.
